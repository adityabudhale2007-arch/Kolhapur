Welcome to Kolhapur

Kolhapur is a historic city known for its temples, royal heritage, spicy food, traditional footwear, and warm local culture.

About the city

A place of history, devotion, and tradition

Kolhapur is located in the southern part of Maharashtra. The city has a rich cultural background and is famous for the Shri Mahalakshmi Temple, historical palaces, wrestling tradition, Kolhapuri chappals, and flavorful cuisine.

Famous places

Popular attractions in Kolhapur

Shri Mahalakshmi Temple

One of the most important temples in Maharashtra, dedicated to Goddess Mahalakshmi.

New Palace

A beautiful palace with museum displays related to Kolhapur's royal family and history.

Rankala Lake

A popular evening spot where people enjoy walks, views, snacks, and peaceful time near the water.

Panhala Fort

A historic hill fort near Kolhapur connected with Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj.

Food and culture

Kolhapur is famous for bold taste and handmade craft.

Kolhapuri misal, tambda rassa, pandhra rassa, and spicy non-veg dishes are loved by visitors. The city is also known for handmade Kolhapuri chappals, traditional jewellery, and kushti culture.
